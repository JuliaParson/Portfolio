Emma's Small Engines Business System

About the Project

This business system was created for a small engine repair business. The goal of this project was to create a site that could create and maintain repair records. I created this site with a group of four other students using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I held the role of designer and presenter. The site is setup so each user will have a specific role and that role allows them to perform certain functions. The admin role can do everything the site allows, the sales role deals with customer relations, and the repairs role records the repair process as they finish each job.
